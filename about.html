About PakStudentFinance

Helping Pakistani students find and finance their higher education — completely free.

Our Mission

Every year, billions of rupees in scholarships go unawarded because students simply do not know they exist, or find the eligibility criteria too confusing to navigate. PakStudentFinance was built to solve this problem.

We are a non-governmental utility platform that centralizes information about scholarships, grants, and education loans available for Pakistani students. Our goal is simple: make financial aid discovery accessible to everyone.

Why are we free?

Education should not come with a paywall. Our tools are completely free, and we do not require users to sign up or create accounts. To keep the servers running and maintain the database, this platform is supported entirely by unobtrusive advertisements.
